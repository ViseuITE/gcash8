Wagering requirements dictate how many times you must wager your bonus funds before withdrawing any winnings. For instance, if you receive a 100 bonus with a 25x wagering requirement, you need to place bets totaling 2,500 (100 x 25) to unlock the bonus.

Tips for Clearing Wagering Requirements

  • Check Withdrawal Rules: Some casinos allow partial withdrawals of winnings as you meet portions of the requirements. Read the terms to see if this applies.
